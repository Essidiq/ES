Berserk" is a dark fantasy manga series written and illustrated by Kentaro Miura. The story follows the protagonist, Guts, a lone mercenary with a tragic past, as he battles through a medieval-inspired world filled with supernatural horrors and political intrigue. Guts wields a massive sword known as Dragonslayer and is marked with a cursed brand that attracts demonic entities. The series is renowned for its intense and graphic storytelling, exploring themes of betrayal, revenge, and the struggle against fate. Miura's intricate artwork and detailed character development contribute to the depth of the narrative, making "Berserk" a complex and emotionally charged experience for readers. The series has gained a dedicated fan base since its debut in 1990, with its mature themes and visceral imagery setting it apart in the realm of manga and dark fantasy storytelling. Tragically, Kentaro Miura passed away in 2021, leaving the future of the series uncertain and fans mourning the loss of a master storyteller.
